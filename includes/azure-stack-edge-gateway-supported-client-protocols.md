Here are the supported protocols for clients accessing your device.

|**Protocol** |**Versions**   |**Notes**  |
|---------|---------|---------|
|SMB    | 2.X, 3.X      | SMB 1 isn't supported.|
|NFS    |3.0       | NFS v4.1 isn't supported.|
